# Quarterly Roadmap 2026–2027 — Projekt Grönland

Single source of truth for phases, goals, key areas, and outcomes. Each phase contributes to autonomy and monetization.

---

## 2026 — Foundation & transition

### Q1 2026 — Platform foundation

**Goal:** Technological independence.

**Outcome:**
- Portal platform core (proprietary architecture) operational
- Own platform live
- API-first integrations layer
- Multi-tenant foundation (tenant as first-class concept)

**Key areas:**
- Portal platform core
- API-first integrations layer
- Authentication & role model
- Tracking & basis analytics
- (Working basis for Product Owner & Product Team)

---

### Q2 2026 — Migration enablement

**Goal:** Safely transfer existing customers.

**Outcome:**
- Migration & parallel-run logic
- Ad & account migration
- Basis for migration created
- Data model for existing customers
- URL & SEO strategy
- First customer pilot migration

**Key areas:**
- Migration & parallel-run logic
- Ad & account migration
- Data model for existing customers
- URL & SEO strategy
- First customer pilot migration

---

### Q3 2026 — HR core entry

**Goal:** Move away from simply posting job-related content.

**Outcome:**
- First true HR SaaS offering
- Application Tracking / Bewerbermanagement (MVP)
- Candidate management
- HR basis workflows
- Clear added value beyond advertisements
- Customer acceptance validated

**Key areas:**
- Application Tracking / Bewerbermanagement (MVP)
- Candidate management
- HR basis workflows
- First true HR SaaS offering

---

### Q4 2026 — Self-service & efficiency

**Goal:** Reduce costs, prepare for scaling.

**Outcome:**
- Platform economically stable
- Basic billing & package logic
- Customer self-service portal (contracts, invoices, advertisements)
- Internal admin tools reduce manual work
- Support reduction features
- Process automation
- Reduced service effort

**Key areas:**
- Customer self-service portal (contracts, invoices, advertisements)
- Basic billing & package logic
- Support reduction features
- Process automation
- Internal admin tools

## 2027 — Monetization & scale

### Q1 2027 — Monetization & payments

**Goal:** Growth without linear costs.

**Outcome:**
- Scalable SaaS revenue model
- Transparent billing
- Payment Service Provider integration
- Add-on & upsell logic
- Measurable ARPU increase
- Higher strategic platform value

**Key areas:**
- Service bundles (e.g. HR + reach)
- Payment Service Provider integration
- Add-on & upsell logic
- Transparent billing
- Scalable SaaS revenue model

---

### Q2 2027 — Vertical HR services

**Goal:** Expand HR SaaS.

**Outcome:**
- Platform becomes HR hub
- Extended ATS functions
- Screening / pre-selection services
- API integration of external HR tools
- Increased lock-in (Erhöhter Lock-in)

**Key areas:**
- Extended ATS functions
- Screening / pre-selection services
- API integration of external HR tools
- Platform as HR hub

---

### Q3 2027 — Portal factory

**Goal:** Attractiveness.

**Outcome:**
- New portals in days instead of months
- Reusable portal templates
- Content & page builder
- White-label capability (reimagined)
- Feature toggles for the Mandant
- High-performance platform
- KPI transparency (ARPU, churn, level of automation)

**Key areas:**
- Reusable portal templates
- Content & page builder
- White-label capability (reimagined)
- Feature toggles for the Mandant
- New portals in days instead of months

---

### Q4 2027 — Readiness & optimization

**Goal:** Future-ready platform.

**Outcome:**
- Documentation & architecture readiness
- Technical & organizational decoupling
- Optional integration paths
- Managed equity value
- Migration capability by mid-2027

**Future-ready criteria:**
- No loss of traffic or revenue
- No loss of traffic or revenue (low-risk migration)
- Client competence (basic)
- Focus on epics / capability levels, not individual features
- White-label technically replaceable

**Key areas:**
- Documentation & architecture readiness
- Technical & organizational decoupling
- Optional integration paths
- Migration readiness by mid-2027
